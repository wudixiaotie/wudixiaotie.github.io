###Open file

    | Name | Command |
    |---|---|
    |open-file | <kbd>C</kbd>-<kbd>x</kbd> <kbd>C</kbd>-<kbd>f</kbd>|
    |open-file-in-other-window | <kbd>C</kbd>-<kbd>x</kbd> <kbd>4</kbd> <kbd>f</kbd>|

|---|---|

|undo-tree-redo | <kbd>M</kbd>-<kbd>_</kbd>|
